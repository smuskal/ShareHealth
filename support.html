Getting Started

First Time Setup:

  1. Download ShareHealth from the App Store
  2. Open the app and grant access to Apple Health when prompted
  3. You're ready to export health data and share steps!

Exporting Health Data (Single Day)

  1. Open ShareHealth
  2. Tap "Export Health Data"
  3. Select the date for your export
  4. Choose your export location (Dropbox, iCloud, or any folder)
  5. Optionally set a default folder for one-tap exports
  6. Tap "Export to Default Folder" or "Export to Other Location..."
  7. Your health metrics will be saved as a CSV file in a YYYY/MM/ subfolder
Tip: Set a default export location for quick one-tap exports. Perfect for regular health tracking!

Historical Export (Date Range)

Export weeks, months, or years of health data at once:

  1. Open ShareHealth
  2. Tap "Historical Export"
  3. The app shows your earliest available health data date
  4. Select your start date (tap "Use" to start from earliest available)
  5. Select your end date
  6. Choose an export folder
  7. Tap "Export X Days"
  8. Monitor progress as each day is exported
  9. When complete, tap "View Log" to see details
Tip: The screen stays awake during export to prevent interruption. For large exports (years of data), keep your iPhone plugged in.

Face Imagery Feature

Associate your face with your health exports so recipients can easily identify whose data they're viewing.

  1. On the Export Health Data screen, toggle "Include Face Imagery"
  2. When prompted, take a selfie
  3. Your photo will be saved to faces/YYYY/MM/ subfolder
  4. The image is stored locally on your device
Privacy Note: Your face photo is stored only on your device and is only shared when you explicitly export your health data.

  • Activity (steps, distance, flights climbed)
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability
  • Sleep analysis, sleep stages, bedtime, and wake time
  • Nutrition (calories, macros, vitamins)
  • Body measurements (weight, height, BMI)
  • Vitals (blood pressure, respiratory rate)
  • Workouts and exercise minutes
  • And many more...

Bedtime and Wake Time

Sleep timing is exported in decimal hours for use in data analysis and machine learning:

  • 22.75 = 10:45 PM
  • 6.5 = 6:30 AM

Sharing Your Steps

  1. Open ShareHealth
  2. Tap "Share Steps"
  3. Select the date you want to share
  4. Adjust the time range using the "Start" and "End" selectors
  5. Add any notes you'd like to include (optional)
  6. Tap "Share Steps"
  7. Choose how to share (Message, Email, etc.)
  8. Select your recipient and send!

Receiving Shared Steps

  1. When someone shares steps with you, you'll receive a link starting with "sharedsteps://"
  2. Tap the link
  3. If this is your first time, you'll need to download ShareHealth and grant Apple Health access
  4. The Import Steps screen will show the shared data
  5. Review the details and tap "Add Steps" to import them to your Apple Health
Tip: You can share steps from any time range within a day - perfect for shared walks or activities!

Troubleshooting

Historical export seems to stop mid-way

Keep the app in the foreground during export. The screen stays awake automatically. After completion, tap "View Log" to see which days succeeded or failed.

Files not appearing in Dropbox

Files are written to Dropbox's local cache first, then uploaded to the cloud in the background. Check the Files app → Dropbox to see local files. Keep the Dropbox app open to speed up uploads.

No sleep data for older years

Native Apple sleep tracking started in September 2020 (iOS 14). Sleep data before that date requires third-party apps like Sleep Cycle or AutoSleep. Your CSVs will still export other metrics; sleep fields will simply be empty.

Common Questions

What format is the health data exported in?

Health data is exported as a CSV (comma-separated values) file, which can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers, or any spreadsheet application.

Where is my face photo stored?

Your face photo is stored locally on your device in the faces/YYYY/MM/ subfolder. It is never uploaded to any server and is only included when you choose to export.

Can I export multiple days at once?

Yes! Use the "Historical Export" feature to export any date range. Each day creates a separate CSV file, organized by year and month.

How do I change my default export folder?

On the Export Health Data screen, tap "Change" next to the export location to select a new default folder.

What do the Bedtime and Wake Time numbers mean?

They are in decimal hours (24-hour format). For example, 22.5 means 10:30 PM, and 7.25 means 7:15 AM. This format is designed for data analysis and machine learning.

Remember: ShareHealth requires Apple Health access to read and write health data. You can check this in your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Getting Started

First Time Setup:

  1. Download ShareHealth from the App Store
  2. Open the app and grant access to Apple Health when prompted
  3. You're ready to export health data and share steps!

Exporting Health Data

  1. Open ShareHealth
  2. Tap "Export Health Data"
  3. Select the date for your export
  4. Choose your export location (Dropbox, iCloud, or any folder)
  5. Optionally set a default folder for one-tap exports
  6. Tap "Export to Default Folder" or "Export to Other Location..."
  7. Your health metrics will be saved as a CSV file
Tip: Set a default export location for quick one-tap exports. Perfect for regular health tracking!

Face Imagery Feature

Associate your face with your health exports so recipients can easily identify whose data they're viewing.

  1. On the Export Health Data screen, toggle "Include Face Imagery"
  2. When prompted, take a selfie or choose an existing photo
  3. Your photo will be included with your health data export
  4. The image is stored locally on your device
Privacy Note: Your face photo is stored only on your device and is only shared when you explicitly export your health data.

Health Metrics Exported

ShareHealth exports 100+ health metrics including:

  • Activity (steps, distance, flights climbed)
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability
  • Sleep analysis and sleep stages
  • Nutrition (calories, macros, vitamins)
  • Body measurements (weight, height, BMI)
  • Vitals (blood pressure, respiratory rate)
  • Workouts and exercise minutes
  • And many more...

Sharing Your Steps

  1. Open ShareHealth
  2. Tap "Share Steps"
  3. Select the date you want to share
  4. Adjust the time range using the "Start" and "End" selectors
  5. Add any notes you'd like to include (optional)
  6. Tap "Share Steps"
  7. Choose how to share (Message, Email, etc.)
  8. Select your recipient and send!

Receiving Shared Steps

  1. When someone shares steps with you, you'll receive a link starting with "sharedsteps://"
  2. Tap the link
  3. If this is your first time, you'll need to download ShareHealth and grant Apple Health access
  4. The Import Steps screen will show the shared data
  5. Review the details and tap "Add Steps" to import them to your Apple Health
Tip: You can share steps from any time range within a day - perfect for shared walks or activities!

Common Questions

What format is the health data exported in?

Health data is exported as a CSV (comma-separated values) file, which can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers, or any spreadsheet application.

Where is my face photo stored?

Your face photo is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to any server and is only included when you choose to export your health data.

Can I share steps from multiple days?

Currently, ShareHealth allows sharing one day at a time. Select the specific day you want to share.

Will importing shared steps affect my existing step data?

No, importing shared steps adds to your Apple Health data without affecting existing records.

Can I edit or delete imported steps?

You can manage all step data through the Apple Health app.

Why isn't my step count updating?

Try switching dates or reopening the app. Make sure Apple Health access is enabled in your device settings.

How do I change my default export folder?

On the Export Health Data screen, tap "Change" next to the export location to select a new default folder.

Remember: ShareHealth requires Apple Health access to read and write health data. You can check this in your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.
